# ChatGPT, from question to working draft

## Thesis

The film is one continuous ChatGPT interaction, not a dashboard montage. A real
composer accepts a practical question, a properly aligned assistant message streams
an answer, Search makes its sources inspectable, and Canvas turns that answer into an
editable Saturday plan. The final lockup resolves to the exact knot, product name,
and one promise.
