# Make your prompt move

## Thesis

ChatGPT creates one prompt seed; Sequences turns it into an ordered, playable
launch film. The same dot persists through every role so the abstract movement is
always causal and legible.

## Palette

Warm white, paper, ink, guides, and Sequences green carry most frames. Sky,
lilac, butter, cobalt, and midnight are rare peak accents. Color follows meaning.

## Typography

Local Montserrat 500–700 carries product and hero language. IBM Plex Mono 500 is
limited to construction labels, scene indices, time, and state metadata.

## Geometry

Circles, rings, hairlines, giant glyphs, rounded film frames, and a single green
path. There is no glass, neon, generic particle cloud, or difficult 3D scene.

## Motion

Orbit, unfurl, and dock. One 2D camera owns world movement. Children own local
motion. Every component is seek-safe on one paused GSAP timeline.

## Review contract

The final composition is 30.333 seconds at 1920 × 1080 and 30 fps. Six macro
beats follow the analyzed violin form: ask, handoff, sequence, build, result,
and lockup. The persistent prompt seed remains causally legible throughout.
