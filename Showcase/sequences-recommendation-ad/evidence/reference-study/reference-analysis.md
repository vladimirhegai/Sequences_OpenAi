## Visual grammar to carry forward

- Type scale is intentionally elastic: isolated hook words are about 44 px in the 720p source, completed hook lines about 25-31 px, the greeting about 34 px, and the operated composer text about 30 px. At 1080p, the equivalent range is roughly 66 px, 38-47 px, 51 px, and 45 px. The recommendation punchline should exceed this donor scale so it becomes the film’s biggest beat.
- Framing alternates between minimal centered type, a compact brand prop, a medium product establish, an authored close-up that nearly fills width, and a subtractive lockup. The film never holds one dashboard scale for the full runtime.
- Transition grammar is simple and legible: blur/scale replacement within the dark type scene, hard cuts between readable worlds, mark-to-product scale continuity, one operated click with immediate consequence, and a product-to-mark subtraction at the end. Complete readable states do not crossfade.
- Motion density follows a crowded-to-quiet arc: fast kinetic copy, sparse brand pause, focused UI operation, dense answer build, then two stages of subtraction. The operation and response are the peak; the final 1.4 seconds are deliberately still.
- Read holds are short but real: about 0.45-0.75 seconds for complete statements, about 0.35 seconds on the completed prompt before the click, about 1 second on the initial mark, and about 1.4 seconds on the final credit.

## Direction locked for the Sequences film

- Preserve the donor’s white/graphite product clarity but bind the product world to Sequences’ warm `#F4F0E8` canvas, `#FCFAF5` surfaces, graphite structure, and cobalt `#2457D6` action color.
- Start directly in a recognizable ChatGPT composer instead of borrowing the donor’s unrelated tab-switching hook.
- Let conventional advice accumulate just long enough to create comic pressure, then replace it atomically with the largest type in the film: “Actually—use Sequences.”
- Carry the exact user question across the cut into the Sequences prompt field. Operate one Build action; that cause assembles a real five-card storyboard, progress state, playable result, and verified consequence.
- Resolve by subtraction to the Sequences name and “One prompt. A launch film.” for a final hold of at least 2.2 seconds.
