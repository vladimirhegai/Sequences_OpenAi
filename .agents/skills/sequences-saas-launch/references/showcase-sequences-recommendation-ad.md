# Showcase capsule: sequences-recommendation-ad

Tags: typing and clicks, interruption narrative, glitch, ChatGPT-to-product handoff, storyboard-to-player, audio sync.

Use when typing and clicking are story-critical, the narrative turns through interruption or recommendation, or a ChatGPT-like request must hand off cleanly into Sequences product proof.

## Transferable lessons

- Treat pointer approach, contact, press, release, and immediate visible consequence as separate measured phases. Put click SFX on contact, not arrival.
- Keep every typing window deterministic and place its SFX exactly over the visible glyph interval.
- Bound an interruption/glitch to the affected conversation blocks and make the narrative replacement legible within a short window.
- Carry the same request into the product, then morph a selected storyboard card from its measured slot into the player before the verified result.

## Known mistakes to avoid

- Do not copy the refusal-to-Sequences storyline, its wording, or unsupported recommendation claims.
- Do not fire the click before the hotspot reaches the target or leave a delay before the control visibly changes.
- Do not let glitch styling obscure the story turn or become a decorative full-screen effect.
- Do not rebuild the selected card at arbitrary coordinates for its player handoff.
