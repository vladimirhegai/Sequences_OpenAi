# Showcase capsule: slack-ad

Tags: SaaS, collaboration, product UI, measured geometry, energetic story, readable holds.

Use when the brief needs a legible friction-to-product-to-proof arc, an operated workspace, motivated camera commits, or high energy without sacrificing read time.

## Transferable lessons

- Build a density arc: disconnected noise accumulates, converges into one focal mark, enters a persistent product surface, proves the payoff, then subtracts toward a quiet lockup.
- Measure interaction and camera destinations. Protect the active message or control at the largest camera pose and hold every landed state long enough to read.
- Keep action and consequence in the same product world. The shared desktop makes the modal, channel, conversation, reaction, and proof feel causally connected.
- Reveal type by measured width so the caret rides the visible edge; separate pointer approach, press, and resulting state.

## Known mistakes to avoid

- Do not copy Slack branding, copy, fictional people, or the complete beat order.
- This historic source predates the current HyperFrames `data-*` contract. Study its craft, not its host structure.
- Do not let a superzoom crop focal copy, rebuild lookalike UI per beat, or replay a persistent element's entrance.
